Our Profile

Integrity in Intermediation

GLOBAL FINANCE PRIME LTDA is a specialized financial services firm located in Sao Jose dos Campos, Sao Paulo. Led by Ederson Lacerda Alves, we operate with a singular focus: to provide transparent, secure, and efficient credit intermediation.

We understand that navigating the financial landscape in Brazil can be complex. As licensed intermediaries, we act as the vital link between individuals or businesses seeking capital and the major banking institutions that provide it.

Our firm is built on the pillars of "Finance and Insurance" expertise. Whether you require check clearing services or complex loan structuring, Global Prime delivers solutions that align with your long-term fiscal goals.
